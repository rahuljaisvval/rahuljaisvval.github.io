Insy - A Job Search App

Insy is an India based job search platform which aspire to provide services for both job seekers as well as employers. Insy wants to offer a simple and easy to use platform both as an app and a responsive website for non tech savvy users.

The Problem and The Goal

Insy has a website but according to user research and reviews. Users are not very happy with the user experience it offers. Most of the people are not very tech savvy and have troubles searching for jobs online both via the app or using a website.

So the goal is to design a new and simple app that will help less tech savvy people find jobs easily.

My Role and Responsibilities

UX designer, leading the app and responsive website design from conception to delivery.

Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, iterating on designs, determining information architecture, and responsive design.

Initial ideation

Taking the time to draft iterations of each screen of the app on paper ensured that the elements that made it to digital wireframes would be well-suited to address user pain points. For the home screen, I prioritized a quick and easy selection process to help users save time.

Digital Wireframes

After ideating and drafting some paper wireframes, I created the initial designs for the Insy app. These designs focused on delivering a simple to use clutter free design that can be easily used with talkback.

Low Fidelity Prototype

To prepare for usability testing, I created a low-fidelity prototype that connected the user flow of selecting and applying for a job.

Usability study: findings

These were the main findings uncovered by the usability study:

  • People want clutter free user interface .
  • People had difficulty knowing when the job was applied for.
  • People preferred clear indications of navigation within the app.

Changes after usability study:

Based on the insights from the usability study, I applied design changes like providing a clear indication navigating through the app. to browse jobs that were saved before user can use “my jobs”.
Additional design changes included adding an ‘Application submitted’ ’to the job page which has been already applied for. and providing a clearer indication of when it was applied.
